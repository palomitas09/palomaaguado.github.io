Alexa

Alexa is a visual tale born under the winter sky. It is the story of a girl whose curiosity is capable of melting ice and finding magic in the frostiest corners of her world.
Through this illustrated story, we explore a child's gaze: the ability to see an entire kingdom where others only see snow, and to find warmth in the silence of a sleeping city.
Written by Carol Vila Micó
Illustrated by Paloma Aguado

Art Direction

Alexa's aesthetic relies on a palette of deep blues and icy whites, seeking a harmony that transmits both peace and wonder in equal measure.
Using generative AI tools, every texture has been refined to evoke the softness of cotton and the brilliance of crystal. Light is not just a visual element, but the guiding thread that leads our protagonist through the winter twilight.

The Story's Heart

"Once upon a time, there was a girl who could hear the whisper of falling snowflakes." This editorial project seeks to connect with the most tender part of our imagination.
More than a story, Alexa is a design exercise that demonstrates how technology can capture the essence of classic storytelling, creating worlds that invite us to dream with our eyes wide open.
Written by Carol Vila Micó · Illustrated by Paloma Aguado
